GPT-5.3-Codex Unveiled

OpenAI announced GPT-5.3-Codex for multi-step software work, spanning testing, docs, and deployment with stronger agent workflows. The release emphasizes self-improving training and broader availability through Codex tools. It positions Codex as a more autonomous collaborator for end-to-end software tasks.

Claude Opus 4.6 Ripples

Anthropic shipped Opus 4.6 with stronger reasoning, deeper tool integrations, and agent teams for parallel work. Investors reacted sharply as the model signals faster disruption of productivity software. The update highlights how quickly AI capabilities are moving into day-to-day office workflows.

Gemini Conductor Debuts

Google released Conductor, a Gemini CLI extension that stores knowledge in Markdown and orchestrates multi-step agent workflows. It targets developers who want repeatable automation that stays readable and versionable. The approach makes agent context easier to audit, share, and commit to git.

4 of 10

Claude Drives Mars Rover

NASA JPL used Anthropic Claude to plan Perseverance rover routes in Rover Markup Language, reducing route-planning time. The test shows agents can handle autonomy where communication delays make human control slow. Engineers still reviewed the routes, but the AI handled much of the planning load.

OpenScholar Beats Big LLMs

OpenScholar is an open-source system for literature reviews that outperforms larger models while keeping citations accurate. It suggests smaller, specialized stacks can be more reliable and cost-efficient for academia. The results push back against the idea that bigger models always win in research workflows.

ChatGPT Share Slips

US app data shows ChatGPT losing share as Gemini grows, while users increasingly keep multiple AI apps. The market is maturing into a multi-tool ecosystem with distinct niches for each platform. Expect more bundling, portability, and switching incentives as competition heats up.

8 of 10

Gemini Adds Chat Imports

Google is adding chat imports so users can move ChatGPT or Claude histories into Gemini. It lowers switching friction even though saved memories are not transferred. The move pushes the ecosystem toward portability over lock-in.

9 of 10

Italy Bans DeepSeek

Italy's regulator blocked DeepSeek over data sovereignty concerns and removed it from app stores. The move signals faster EU enforcement and wider scrutiny for cross-border AI providers. It also pressures vendors to prove compliance before scaling in Europe.

10 of 10

Perplexity Model Council

Perplexity launched Model Council to run queries through GPT, Claude, and Gemini in parallel and synthesize the results. It is aimed at higher-confidence research where disagreements are surfaced, not hidden. The feature positions multi-model verification as a premium research capability.
